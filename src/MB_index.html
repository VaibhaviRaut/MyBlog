"Weave your Words to create your own Legacy"

This is my motto. I am here in this world to do something, something that leaves a mark on the sands of time. Blessed with the art to use words I want to make a difference in the world. I want to create my own Identity, my own Legacy. I know I am still a fledgling but this is my first step out of my nest into a world of new ventures and new things to learn. Thank you, WordPress for providing me with this platform to showcase my talents and share it with others and to know others' reviews. I hope you like my poems. Hi there! Vaibhavi here.


Hi I'm Vaibhavi Raut. I am 22 years old and my hobby is to write. I started writing when I was 13, although it was mostly songs! I won my first poetry competition when I was in school. One of my best poems, "Patriotism" won the first prize in an inter-house school competition. And since then I felt more inspired and motivated to write. I am still a beginner but I hope to write a peice that would encourage others as well to join me on this journey. I written many songs, poems and short blogs and how I felt each time. Most of my work relies on how I what emotion I felt at the moment but many were inspired from people or situations that moved my heart. Of course, capturing every detail is difficult but I plan to hone my skills where I could give my reader the power to vividly imagine and feel the same emotions and my journey so far. Appreciation is one of the key factors to motivate mt but I would love if my readers could share their honest critique as well!
