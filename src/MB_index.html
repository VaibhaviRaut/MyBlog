Blessed with the art to use words I want to share everything I've experienced. Wanderer at heart and want to venture into the world to learn new things. Hi there! Vaibhavi here.


Hey there! I’m Vaibhavi. Nice to meet you. I’m 22 years old and one of my hobbies is to write. I started out with song writing and composing music but one day my teacher caught me writing songs in class and without me knowing submitted my name for a poetry competition. “Patriotism” was the first ever poem I wrote and never in my wildest dreams would I have ever imagined winning the first place. Ever since then I started writing more and more. Although most of them turn out to be songs :P I am currently a master’s student and even though life has been hectic writing has always been my therapy. A friend that encouraged me to try out newer hobbies like playing musical instruments and learning new languages like Japanese. Now, I play Guitar and Ukulele but would love to learn other instruments!
Well, that's about me, but I wanna know about you! Feel free to browse through my works and share your thoughts and story with me!

P.S.: Stay Healthy. Stay Safe. With love, Vaibhavi!
